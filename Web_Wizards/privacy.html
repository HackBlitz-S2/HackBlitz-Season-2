SkillMatch Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

This Privacy Policy describes how SkillMatch ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our career guidance and portfolio building platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Demographic information (age, location, education level)
  • Professional information (work history, skills, certifications)
  • Account credentials (username and password)

Usage Data

  • Career interests and preferences
  • Platform interaction data (pages visited, features used)
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and approximate location

Portfolio Content

  • Projects you upload to your portfolio
  • Work samples and descriptions
  • Testimonials and recommendations

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized career recommendations
  • To suggest relevant learning resources
  • To build and display your professional portfolio
  • To improve and optimize our platform
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To analyze usage trends and market demands

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Your Consent: When you choose to make your portfolio public or share it with specific employers
  • Service Providers: With third parties who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls for staff

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: You can update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another service
  • Opt-out: You can opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@skillmatch.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Deliver personalized content

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
